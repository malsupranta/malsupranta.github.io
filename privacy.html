Privacy Policy for Transit

Last updated: 15.2.2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile application Transit ("App") handles user information. The App operates primarily offline. Transit does not collect, transmit, store, or share personal data on external servers, except as required for integrated advertising services.

2. Data Collection

Transit does not require account registration and does not directly collect personal information.

  • No account registration is required
  • No personal identifiers are collected by the developer
  • No developer-operated analytics services are used
  • User-generated data remains on the user’s device

The App contains advertisements. Advertising providers may collect limited device data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

3. Local Data Storage

Any data entered into or generated by Transit is stored exclusively on the user’s device.

  • No uploading of user-created content
  • No remote access to local data
  • No cloud synchronization

Users are responsible for managing, backing up, or deleting their locally stored data.

4. Permissions

Transit only requests permissions necessary for core functionality.

The App does not access:

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Microphone
  • External accounts

Advertising providers may access limited device information as required for ad delivery.

5. Third-Party Services

Transit integrates third-party advertising services to display ads. These providers may process certain device-related information to serve advertisements. Please refer to the respective advertising provider’s privacy policy for further details.

6. Children’s Privacy

Transit is rated for general audiences (AB 0). The App does not knowingly collect personal data. Advertising providers may process limited data in accordance with their own policies.

7. Data Security

Since user-generated data is stored locally, data security depends on the user’s device security. Users are encouraged to enable PIN protection, passwords, or biometric authentication.

8. International Users

As Transit does not directly collect personal data, international data transfer regulations apply primarily to integrated advertising services.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted within the App or on its official distribution page with an updated revision date.
